Need Help?

Find answers to common questions and learn how to use the tools available on Toolkit.

How do I use PDF tools?

Our PDF tools allow you to merge, split, and convert PDF files effortlessly. Simply upload your file, choose the desired action (merge, split, or convert), and download the result for free.

How can I reduce the size of my images?

You can easily resize or compress images using our free image tools. Upload your image, select the size reduction or compression option, and download the optimized image.

What text-related services do you offer?

Our text tools let you convert between various text formats, remove formatting, and much more. Upload your text file, choose the action you want, and download the updated file.

Do I need to create an account to use the tools?

No, you do not need to create an account. All the tools are available to use for free, without any sign-up required.
